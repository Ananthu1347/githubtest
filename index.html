LATTE

A latte is like a cozy hug in a cup, with velvety steamed milk swirling into rich, aromatic espresso.

It's a harmonious blend of bold coffee and creamy sweetness, creating a smooth and comforting experience with every sip. Whether enjoyed as a morning ritual or a midday treat, a well-made latte is a delightful balance of flavors and textures that soothes the senses and warms the soul.

FRAPPUCCINO

A Frappuccino is a blended coffee drink (or a coffee-flavored base) with ice and other ingredients like flavored syrups, milk, and whipped cream. It's known for its smooth, icy texture and sweet, indulgent flavors.

Frappuccinos come in a variety of flavors, including classic coffee, mocha, caramel, and vanilla, among others. They can also be customized with different milk options, toppings, and additional flavors to suit individual preferences.

MACCHIATO

A macchiato is a coffee beverage that consists of a shot of espresso "stained" with a small amount of steamed milk or milk foam. The term "macchiato" means "stained" or "spotted" in Italian, referring to the way the milk stains the espresso.

Macchiatos are typically served in small cups and are known for their strong coffee flavor with just a hint of sweetness from the milk.

MOCHA CAPACCINO

A mocha cappuccino combines the rich, chocolatey flavors of a mocha with the creamy texture of a cappuccino. The taste is a delightful balance of bitter espresso, sweet chocolate, and creamy milk foam. The chocolate syrup adds a sweetness that complements the bitterness of the espresso, while the steamed milk and foam create a smooth, velvety texture.

AFFOGATO

Affogato is a simple yet elegant Italian dessert that combines two beloved elements: espresso and gelato (or ice cream). The word "affogato" translates to "drowned" in Italian, referring to the act of pouring a shot of hot espresso over a scoop of cold gelato or ice cream.

The result is a delightful contrast of temperatures and textures, with the hot espresso melting the gelato slightly to create a creamy, coffee-infused treat. The bitterness of the espresso balances beautifully with the sweetness of the gelato, making it a harmonious and satisfying dessert experience.
